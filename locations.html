Locations

Orakei
Wharf

Orakei Wharf, close to the city, offers easy access and scenic views of the Waitemata Harbour. Snapper and kahawai are commonly caught here, making it ideal for casual and beginner anglers. Tidy and well-maintained, it's a great legal option for urban fishing.

Waiheke Island

Waiheke Islan offers a more remote and peaceful fishing experience. Its rocky shorelines and wharves attract snapper, john dory, and kingfish. Be sure to follow local rules as it is a special environment worth preserving for future generations.

Murrays Bay Wharf

Located along the North Shore, Murrays Bay Wharf is known for calm waters and a relaxed vibe. It's a great spot for catching sprats, mackerel, and even the occasional trevally. A family-friendly location that supports responsible shoreline fishing.

Auckland Harbour

As the heart of the city's waterfront, Auckland Harbour offers diverse fishing opportunities. From snapper to kingfish, the variety is strong—but so are the regulations. Stick to legal zones and enjoy an iconic urban fishing experience.

Devonport Wharf

With stunning views of the harbour and Rangitoto, Devonport Wharf is a picturesque and accessible spot. It's ideal for catching small fish like yellowtail and jack mackerel—perfect for beginners learning the ropes of legal fishing.

Birkenhead Wharf

Conveniently located with ferry access, Birkenhead Wharf is a local favourite for evening fishing. Anglers often reel in snapper and baitfish such as pilchards on this wharf. A reliable legal spot just minutes from central Auckland.

Point England Reserve

Set against grassy reserves and open space, Point England is perfect for land-based fishing. Kahawai and mullet are frequent catches here. This east-side gem supports community-led efforts to fish legally and sustainably.

Mangere Bridge

Māngere Bridge offers a blend of strong tidal flows and fish-rich waters. It's a hot spot for kahawai and eels, with plenty of space for land-based fishing. A southern location known for community and conservation focus.
