Murrays Bay Wharf

Murrays Bay Wharf is located in the suburb of Murrays Bay on Auckland's North Shore, New Zealand. The wharf is situated at the eastern end of Murrays Bay Beach, accessible via Beach Road. It's opposite to the Murrays Bay Sailing Club at 511 Beach Road, Auckland 0630.
