Murrays Bay Wharf

Murrays Bay Wharf is located in the suburb of Murrays Bay on Auckland's North Shore, New Zealand. The wharf is situated at the eastern end of Murrays Bay Beach, accessible via Beach Road. It's opposite the Murrays Bay Sailing Club at 511 Beach Road, Auckland 0630.

Murrays Bay

Birkenhead Wharf

Birkenhead Wharf is at 1 Hinemoa Street, Birkenhead, Auckland 0626, New Zealand. Situated on Auckland's North Shore, it serves as a ferry terminal connecting the suburb of Birkenhead to downtown Auckland via the Waitematā Harbour.

Birkenhead

Devonport Wharf

Devonport Wharf is at Queens Parade, Devonport, Auckland 0624, New Zealand. Situated on Auckland's North Shore, it serves as a historic ferry terminal connecting the seaside village of Devonport to downtown Auckland via the Waitematā Harbour.

Devonport

Auckland Harbour Bridge

The Auckland Harbour Bridge fishing spot (western side) is located near Westhaven Drive, St Marys Bay, Auckland 1011, New Zealand. Situated on the western side of the Harbour Bridge, this area is popular for land-based fishing and provides access to Westhaven Marina and the surrounding coastal walkway.

Auckland Harbour Bridge

Waiheke Island

Waiheke Island is located in the Hauraki Gulf, approximately 21.5 kilometers northeast of Auckland CBD, New Zealand. Accessible via ferry from downtown Auckland, the island is known for its vineyards, beaches, art scene, and relaxed coastal lifestyle.

Waiheke Island

Orakei Wharf

Ōrākei Wharf is located at Tamaki Drive, Ōrākei, Auckland 1071, New Zealand. Positioned at the eastern end of Okahu Bay, the wharf is a popular recreational fishing and sightseeing spot, offering panoramic views across the Waitematā Harbour.

Orakei

Point England Reserve

Point England Reserve is located at Point England Road, Glen Innes, Auckland 1072, New Zealand. Situated along the Tamaki River, the reserve features coastal walking trails, open fields, and access to estuarine wildlife and scenic views of the eastern bays.

Point England Reserve

Māngere Bridge

Māngere Bridge is located at Bridge Street, Māngere Bridge, Auckland 2022, New Zealand. Connecting Māngere and Onehunga over the Manukau Harbour, the bridge and its surrounding area are known for walking and cycling paths, as well as proximity to Ambury Regional Park and waterfront fishing spots.

Mangere Bridge
