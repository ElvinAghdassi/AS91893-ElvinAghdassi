About Us

We are a local initiative supported by Auckland Council, dedicated to promoting sustainable and legal fishing across the Auckland region. Our mission is to guide local fishermen, both experienced and new, towards approved fishing locations that help preserve marine life and protect the coastal ecosystem. We believe in making responsible fishing simple and accessible.

Our platform offers detailed insights into each legal fishing spot, including the types of fish commonly found there, helpful tips, and visuals that bring each location to life. By highlighting these spots, we aim to reduce illegal fishing activity while fostering a community that values conservation and respect for nature.
